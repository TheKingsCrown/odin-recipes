Beef Ramen

Overview

Description

A quick and easy chicken noodle soup loaded with chicken flavor, perfectly tender vegetables, whole-wheat pasta
and a dose of freshness from lemon and dill. Don’t let the simple ingredient list and easy method fool you—this soup
is loaded with comfort, like every chicken noodle soup should be!

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Ground Coriander
  • Celery Seeds (optional)
  • Mirepoix
  • Low-Sodium Chicken Broth
  • Whole Wheat Pasta
  • Lemon
  • Dill

Steps

  1. Cook the ground chicken
  2. Add mirepoix
  3. Add broth
  4. Set colander and cook pasta
  5. Stire cooked chicken
  6. Ladle soup over noodles
